Data Collection


Overall, we collected Cancer data, health condition and income data as well as economic and agricultural factors data. We gathered this four datasets from distinct data sources using different methods.


For the United States Cancer Statistics Data, we downloaded the zip of excel files on the CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention) [1]. It contains the incidence count and rate(overall and by sex) as well as the mortality count and rate(overall and by sex) at both country level and state level from year 1999 to 2015.


For the Health Condition Data, it is the annual report from the America’s Health Rankings - United Health Foundation [2]. We downloaded the excel files of each year’s annual reports from year 1999 to 2015 and joined all the files into an overall big excel table. It has various measure name such as smoking, obesity, median household income, etc.. In addition, for each measure, there are state name, rank, and value associated with it.


The economic data is one of the subsection from the national data on the BEA (Bureau of Economic Analysis) [3] website.


Finally, for the agricultural data, we obtained it from the national agricultural statistics service on USDA(United States Department of Agriculture) [4] by web scraping. The data contains state name, commodity name, value, and domain such as chemical and fertilizer for year 1997, 2002, 2007, 2012, and 2013.
