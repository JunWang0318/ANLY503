Exploratory Data Analysis


In Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), we propose five sub-questions.


Sub-Question 1: What is the overall trending for the the age adjusted cancer incidence rate, mortality rate and death rate?


Sub-Question 2: What are the top 3 cancer types in the United States? What characteristics do they have?


Sub-Question 3: Is there a relationship between the cancer rate and the economic activities?


Sub-Question 4: Is there a relationship between the age adjusted cancer rate and the income, smoking, diabetes, and obesity?


Sub-Question 5: Could chemical use correlate with cancers?
